Fresher Party

Fresher Party at Nitra Technical Campus
The fresher party at Nitra Technical Campus is a lively event that warmly welcomes new students into the campus community. Sport Fest

Golden Jubilee

NITRA is celebrating its Golden Jubilee, marking 50 years of excellence in education, innovation, and student success. The milestone is commemorated with a series of events, competitions, and cultural activities that unite students, alumni, and faculty. Highlights include awards and cash prizes for outstanding achievements in academics, sports, and extracurriculars. The celebrations and updates are featured on Nitra Mitra, allowing everyone to participate in and share the joy of this historic occasion. Coding Extravanganza

CodSoc, the Coding Society of NITRA Technical Campus, recently held a vibrant Coding Extravaganza. The event featured a Quiz Competition, Coding Contest, and Poster Competition, each designed to engage and challenge students. Participants showcased their coding knowledge, problem-solving skills, and creativity. Prizes and certificates were awarded to top performers, recognizing their hard work and talent. Faculty also shared insights on the importance of coding skills in today’s tech-driven world, inspiring students to further their engagement. The Coding Extravaganza successfully promoted innovation, collaboration, and the significance of coding in academic and professional growth.

About us

Nitra Mitra is a student-driven platform developed by Vaibhav Babele and Ghanshyam Prajapati of NITRA Technical Campus. Our goal is to simplify academic life for AKTU students by offering easy access to essential resources like notes, previous semester papers, and more. With a focus on user-friendly design and relevant content, Nitra Mitra is tailored to meet the specific needs of students, providing them with the tools they need to excel in their studies. We are committed to continuously enhancing the platform to better serve the academic community.
